About Me

I started my professional career in marketing four years ago at a social media agency. Several months later, I landed a dream job in Copenhagen, Denmark with a study abroad company and I was able to gain experience in several aspects of marketing, in addition to getting my first taste of user experience design.

When I returned to the States, I began working in an inbound marketing role, for a large EdTech startup, where I created content strategies to strengthen clients' search rankings. Eventually I decided that user experience design needed a more pivotal role in my career, so I enrolled in General Assembly's first User Experience Design Immersive program in Washington, DC to sharpen my skills in June 2014.

I began working with a freelance startup client soon after finishing the program at General Assembly. Shortly after landing a freelance gig I began working full-time in the transportation mission area at Noblis for a federal client.

In October, I enrolled in Udacity's Front-End Wed Development Nanodegree program to strengthen my front-end coding skills. In my spare time, I enjoy wandering through new cities as often as my budget will allow, discovering new music and binge-watching one show at a time on Netflix.
